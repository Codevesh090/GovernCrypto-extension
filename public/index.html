Features

From AI-powered summaries to native voice conversations — GovernCrypto makes DAO participation effortless.

⚡
AI Proposal Summaries
Instant Mistral AI summaries. Understand what you're voting on in seconds, not hours.
🎙️
Voice AI Assistant
Say "Hey" to ask questions about any proposal by voice. Powered by ElevenLabs with native accent voices.
🌐
15 Languages
Summaries and voice in English, Hindi, Hinglish, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese and more.

Gasless Voting
Cast votes via EIP-712 signing through Snapshot. No gas fees. No blockchain transactions.

WalletConnect

🎨
4 Themes

How it works

From install to vote
in under 2 minutes

Designed to get out of your way and let you focus on making informed governance decisions.

01 — Install
Load the Extension
Download from GitHub and load as unpacked Chrome extension. 30 seconds.
02 — Setup
Enter API Keys
Add your Mistral AI and ElevenLabs API keys. Stored locally, never shared.
03 — Connect
Connect Wallet
Choose from MetaMask, Backpack, Coinbase or any WalletConnect wallet.
04 — Explore
Browse Proposals
See active proposals across all DAOs with AI summaries ready instantly.
05 — Ask
Ask by Voice
Say "Hey" to start a voice conversation about any proposal in your language.
06 — Vote
Cast Your Vote
Vote Yes or No with one click. Gasless EIP-712 signing via Snapshot.
