Features

Everything you need to
govern smarter

From AI-powered summaries to native voice conversations — GovernCrypto makes DAO participation effortless.

⚡
AI Proposal Summaries
Instant Mistral AI summaries of any proposal. Understand what you're voting on in seconds — not hours.
🎙️
Voice AI Assistant
Say "Hey" to ask questions about any proposal by voice. Powered by ElevenLabs with native accent voices.
🌐
15 Languages
Summaries and voice responses in English, Hindi, Hinglish, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese and more.
🗳️
Gasless Voting
Cast votes directly via EIP-712 signing through Snapshot. No gas fees. No blockchain transactions.
🔗
WalletConnect
Connect MetaMask, Backpack, Coinbase or any of 600+ wallets via WalletConnect v2.
🎨
4 Themes
Dark, Midnight, Ocean, and Light themes. Your governance dashboard, your style.
🔊
Sound Effects
Professional UI sounds for every interaction. Toggle on or off in voice settings.
📡
Offline Detection
Smart offline banner keeps you informed when your connection drops. Never miss a vote deadline.
🔒
Privacy First
API keys stored locally in chrome.storage. Never sent to any server other than the respective APIs.

How it works

From install to vote
in under 2 minutes

GovernCrypto is designed to get out of your way and let you focus on what matters — making informed governance decisions.

01 — Install
Load the Extension
Download from GitHub and load as an unpacked Chrome extension. Takes 30 seconds.
02 — Setup
Enter API Keys
Add your Mistral AI and ElevenLabs API keys. Stored locally, never shared.
03 — Connect
Connect Your Wallet
Choose from MetaMask, Backpack, Coinbase or any WalletConnect wallet.
04 — Explore
Browse Proposals
See active proposals across all supported DAOs with AI summaries ready instantly.
05 — Ask
Ask by Voice
Say "Hey" to start a voice conversation about any proposal in your language.
06 — Vote
Cast Your Vote
Vote Yes or No with a single click. Gasless EIP-712 signing via Snapshot.
